Truth and Privacy for All

CPU Mineable

Verus uses VerusHash 2.0, a new cryptographic hash algorithm allowing mining with x64 CPUs while equalizing GPUs and offering a credible ASIC solution.

Proof of Power

With provable 51% hash attack immunity, Verus' 50% proof of work/50% proof of stake consensus algorithm provides unprecedented blockchain security.

Privacy

Verus leverages zk-SNARK zero-knowledge proofs in a modified Zcash Sapling fork for optional private addresses that are completely opaque to the outside world.

Vision

Read about the tech and vision for Verus in the Vision Paper and Phase I White Paper. Verus will introduce Public Blockchains as a Service (PBaaS).

Phase 1

This phase is completed with all goals achieved:

  • Verus Mainnet Fair Launch
  • Verushash CPU/Q-secure algorithm
  • zk-SNARK privacy
  • 50/50 PoW/PoS

Phase 2

Mobile wallets for iOS and Android have been developed and are now at Beta-testing stage.

We are currently developing PBaas. The first PBaaS autochains have been launched on the public testnet and tested extensively with the help of our community. It is all looking very good, but still needs work. We're now working on minor inconveniences and implementing further enhancements, before running a second round of testing. (Check our discord for up-to-date information)

VerusPay, the independent payment gateway plugin for Wordpress ecommerce sites has been developed up to version 0.3.0 and now supports payments in VRSC (Transparent & Private), ARRR (Private) and KMD (Transparent). In addition to that it is now possible for the store owner to withdraw funds to a predefined address with the click of a button. For more information connect to ourdiscord.
