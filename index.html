CPU Mineable

Verus uses a new hash algorithm - VerusHash. VerusHash is optimized for CPUs to combat centralization from specialized mining hardware.

PoW/PoS/dPoW

Verus uses a novel Proof-of-Stake system along side Proof-of-Work, and incorporates Komodo's dPoW for a secure and highly attack resistant chain.

Privacy

Verus leverages zk-SNARK zero-knowledge proofs for optional private addresses that are completely opaque to the outside world.

Vision

Read about the tech and vision for Verus in the Vision Paper and Phase I White Paper. Verus will introduce Public Blockchains as a Service (PBaaS).
